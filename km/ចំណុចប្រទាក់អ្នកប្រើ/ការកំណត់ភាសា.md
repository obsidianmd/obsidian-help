The Obsidian interface has been translated to various languages by [[ឥណទាន#អ្នកបកប្រែ|our amazing volunteer translators]]. You can change the interface language either in **[[ការកំណត់]]** ![[lucide-settings.svg#icon]] → **General** or when you create a new vault.

Is your language missing from the list? Are you seeing untranslated snippets of text? [[ការបកប្រែ|Help us translate Obsidian into your language]].
