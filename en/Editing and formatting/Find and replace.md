Use Find and Replace to search inside the active note. To search across your vault, use [[Search]].

## Open Find and Replace

- To find text in the active note, open the [[Command palette]] and select **Search current file**.
- To find and replace text in the active note, open the [[Command palette]] and select **Search & replace in current file**.
- You can also select **More options** ![[lucide-more-horizontal.svg#icon]] in the upper-right corner of the note, and then select **Find** or **Replace**.

> [!tip]- Keyboard shortcuts
> You can assign [[Hotkeys|keyboard shortcuts]] to **Search current file** and **Search & replace in current file**.

## Find matches

1. Open **Find**.
2. Enter the text you want to search for.
3. Select **Previous** or **Next** to move between matches.
4. Select **Find all** to select every match in the active note.

## Replace matches

1. Open **Replace**.
2. In the **Find** field, enter the text you want to replace.
3. In the **Replace** field, enter the replacement text.
4. Select **Replace** to replace the current match, or **Replace all** to replace every match in the active note.
